# Host-Risk Caveat Prompt

Identify host-risk caveats that should overlay relationship evidence. Host-risk
caveats include shared disease, pest, nematode, or pathogen host status that may
make an otherwise compatible or beneficial relationship risky in a region,
season, or production system.

Emit caveats as relationship claims using a `host_group` node when the evidence
is aggregate. Keep them as overlays; do not use them only as fallback evidence
after direct crop evidence is missing.
