Wheat Growth Stages and Monitoring Guide

The document provides an overview of crop monitoring and wheat growth stages using the Zadoks Decimal Code system. It describes the key growth stages of wheat from emergence through seedling growth, tillering, stem elongation, heading, flowering and ripening. Figures and tables are included to illustrate the growth stages and typical timing of stages for different wheat varieties and sowing dates in southern Australia. The purpose of monitoring growth stages is to inform strategic and tactical crop management decisions.
