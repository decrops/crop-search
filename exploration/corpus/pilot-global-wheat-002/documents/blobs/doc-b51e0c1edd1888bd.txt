The progress of science is only possible by the exchange of ideas and the discussion of theories and paradigms. Our mission is to provide a new environment for the publication of scientific articles.

Mission: Advance scientific progress through free exchange of ideas. Offer researchers a sustainable open-access platform.

We want to express our deepest gratitude to all authors, editors, reviewers, and readers who have supported Librello over the years. Your trust in our mission—to provide truly free and open access to scientific knowledge—has been the foundation of our work and success.

After over a decade of commitment to open science, we have made the difficult decision to pause all new publications indefinitely. This decision was not made lightly, but we believe it is the right step for the future of the organization and the evolving landscape of academic publishing.

While our journals will remain accessible for reference and archival purposes, we encourage the community to continue advocating for open-access publishing and the values we have shared.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Your contributions have helped shape a more open, transparent, and collaborative scientific world.
